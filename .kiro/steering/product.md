# Product Overview

FoodBridge AI is a campus food access platform that reduces food waste and improves student access to affordable meals. The platform connects students with food providers (dining halls, clubs, restaurants, event organizers) through a centralized web portal.

## Core Capabilities

- Students discover and reserve surplus food listings
- Students book pantry appointments and manage orders
- Providers donate surplus food and manage listings
- Embedded AI assistant helps users through natural conversation
- Preference learning system provides personalized recommendations

## User Roles

- **Student**: Discovers food, makes reservations, books pantry appointments
- **Provider**: Creates food listings, manages donations, tracks reservations
- **Admin**: Manages pantry inventory and system configuration

## Key Features

- Food listing search with dietary filters
- Reservation system with quantity tracking
- Pantry appointment booking
- AI assistant with tool execution capabilities
- Smart pantry cart generation based on user history
- Notification system for food alerts and confirmations
- Dining deals and event food listings
- Volunteer coordination

## AI Assistant

The conversational AI assistant interprets natural language and executes structured tools to:
- Search food listings
- Reserve meals
- Book pantry appointments
- Retrieve notifications and dining deals
- Generate smart pantry carts from user history
- Suggest recipes using pantry items

The assistant learns user preferences over time (dietary restrictions, frequent items, preferred restaurants) to provide personalized recommendations.
